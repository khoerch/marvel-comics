Helpful Links

There are lots of great resources around the web to learn more about comics and to get in on the discussion.

  • Comic Book Herald: Great resource for reading lists of events and characters
  • r/marvel: A subreddit for everything Marvel!
  • r/comicbooks: Another subreddit for the wider world of comics!
  • Variant YouTube Channel: Great channel for quick histories on your favorite characters
